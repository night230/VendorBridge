How It Works

Procurement in 7 Steps

A fully structured workflow from RFQ to invoice, built for accountability.

01

Create RFQ

Procurement officer creates a request with product specs, quantity, and vendor assignment.

02

Vendors Quote

Assigned vendors receive invitations and submit their pricing and delivery quotations.

03

Compare

Procurement team compares all quotations side-by-side and selects the best vendor.

04

Approval

Manager reviews and approves or rejects the selected quotation with remarks.

05

Purchase Order

Approved quotation auto-generates a numbered Purchase Order with all details.

06

Invoice

Invoice is generated from the PO — ready to print, download as PDF, or email.

07

Track & Report

All activities logged in audit trail. Analytics report on vendor performance and spend.
